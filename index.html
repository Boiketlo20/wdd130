South Africa

South Africa is a diverse and vibrant country located at the southernmost tip of Africa, bordered by the Atlantic and Indian Oceans. Known as the "Rainbow Nation" for its rich cultural and ethnic diversity, it has 11 official languages, including Zulu, Xhosa, Afrikaans, and English. South Africa boasts stunning landscapes, from rugged coastlines and majestic mountains to vast savannas and deserts.
